About Me!

Hello! I'm Sally (or Yubin) and currently, I am a senior majoring in Computer Science degree with a minor in Creative Technology & Design at the University of Colorado at Boulder. I'm very interested in working as a Software Engineer after graduation and for long term goal, I am seeking to become a full-stack engineer. I was born in Daegu, South Korea, and came to the United States alone when I was 12 years old. One of my greatest strength is adaptability. To survive, I had to learn and challenge myself to adapt to any environment quickly, and also, I had to make my own network from scratch. I believe my adaptability can let me uniquely contribute to any environment for my career.



Currently, I'm a Research Lab Assistant at CUPLV (Programming Languages And Verification at University of Colorado - Boulder) since Feb 2021. I’ve been a passionate student who’s seeking a challenging but rewarding experience that pushes me out of my comfort zone. To pursue a wider range of knowledge that I can’t learn from a university, I founded a club called HAWC aka. ‘Hacking for Asian Women Crew’ in sophomore.

I am looking forward to learn more various tools and technologies in programming and excited to begin a new journey after May 2022!
