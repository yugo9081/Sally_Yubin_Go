About Me!

My name is Sally and currently, I am a senior majoring in CS at the University of Colorado at Boulder. I chose this major because being a programmer seems very cool and it can lead to a great career option. I’ve been a passionate student who’s seeking a challenging but rewarding experience that pushes me out of my comfort zone. To pursue a wider range of knowledge that I can’t learn from a university, I founded a club called HAWC aka. Hacking for Asian Women Crew in sophomore year. Not just awarded as the best project from cs course, I have three work experiences in three different fields such as robotics, web development, and hardware. With these experiences in various fields, I believe It will definitely help me to communicate with various teams and can apply this knowledge to work.
